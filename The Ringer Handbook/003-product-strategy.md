# Product Strategy

## Product Thesis

Ringer coordinates people who want to play sport.

## Mission

Help people play more sport, more often, with people they trust.

## Five Product Pillars

1. Discover Opportunity
2. Build Trust
3. Reduce Coordination
4. Strengthen Communities
5. Encourage Participation

## Product Principles

- Recommendations over search.
- People before logistics.
- Confidence over convenience.
- Organisers are power users.

## North Star Metric

Games Played.

## The WhatsApp Test

Every feature must answer why people wouldn't simply use WhatsApp instead.

## Product Boundaries

Ringer is not a social media platform, fitness tracker, booking marketplace or league management tool.
